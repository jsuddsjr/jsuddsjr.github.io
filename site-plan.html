Temple Inn & Suites Site Plan

John Sudds

Overview

Purpose

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain headquartered in Bethesda, MD that builds and manages specialized hotel accommodations near temple locations of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints around the world. The overall goal of the website project is to promote and provide information about the specialized services that this hotel chain offers. The website should reflect the Church culture and be designed to support a temple oriented theme as well as the concept of being a haven of comfort and accessibility.

Audience

The owner-operated company caters to temple workers, patrons, and their families by providing specialized services and accommodations that meet the needs of guests both young and old. The site must be easy to navigate and accessible to those with low vision. Temple Inn guests will include:

  • Members of the church of all ages
  • Physically active families with small children
  • Newly married couples
  • Older guests with reduced mobility
  • Church missionaries and employees
  • Non-members who know little about the church or temples

Branding

Website Logo

The image includes a sun stone design from the original Nauvoo temple, a symbol instantly recognized by members of the church. The logo's friendly design welcomes guests and reminds them of good mornings ahead. The large ampersand (&) hints at the specialized amenities that are included with their stay.

Typography

Heading Font: IM Fell French Canon

The custom ligatures and kerning of this font give it an old-world style that evokes early American history.

Paragraph Font: Domine

This font is easy to read and complements the heading font well.
